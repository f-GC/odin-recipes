Chili

Description

This simple 5-ingredient chili recipe is cheap, fast and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can chili beans, not drained
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and cook the ground beef until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Stir in kidney beans, chili beans, tomato sauce and chili powder, and simmer un til thickened, about 15 minutes.
  3. Season to taste with salt and pepper
